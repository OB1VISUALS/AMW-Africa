PAPA LOU ENGLE

Over the last 25 years, Papa Lou has stewarded Joel 2 gatherings that contended for repentance, renewal, and the prophetic destiny of nations.

THE BIRTH OF A Million Women Africa

In 2024, that legacy crescendoed into the launch of A Million Women (AMW) on October 12, the Day of Atonement. This global call summoned Esthers and Mordecais to rise and contend for their nations.

Out of this divine moment, A Million Women Africa (AMWA) was born — under the continental leadership of Apostle Isi Igenegba. Through solemn assemblies, equipping sessions, and strategic intercession across the nations of Africa and the diaspora, AMWA aims to equip the Body of Christ in prophetic intercession and facilitate national awakening for the fulfillment of prophetic destiny and the securing of a righteous future for the next generation.

KAIROS AFRICA & THE ROAD TO 2026

In 2025, on the Day of Atonement, Kairos Africa brought together leaders and intercessors from 35 African nations in a unified cry of repentance and consecration. Representatives stood in the gap for their nations, seeking God's mercy and direction during a season of growing global instability. This moment marked a shared continental posture before the Lord.

OUR VISION & MISSION

VISION

As a parachurch prayer movement, A Million Women Africa exists to mobilize women and men as modern-day Esthers and Mordecais — calling nations to prayer, fasting, and spiritual awakening for the fulfillment of their prophetic destiny, and to secure a future for the next generation.

MISSION

A Million Women Africa exists to mobilize women and men as modern-day Esthers and Mordecais — awakening nations to prayer, fasting, and spiritual transformation through:

  • —Monthly prayer and training
  • —Annual gatherings centered around the Day of Atonement
  • —National solemn assemblies

CORE VALUES

1

Israel Is Central

We affirm that Israel remains central to God's redemptive purposes and prophetic timeline. Our prayers honor God's covenantal faithfulness, recognizing that the destiny of the nations is inseparably connected to His plans for Israel. (Joel 3; Romans 11)

2

Nations Belong to Jesus

Jesus is the rightful King of the nations, and every people group exists within His sovereign design. We contend in prayer for nations to align with heaven's purposes and fulfill their God-given destinies. (Psalm 2)

3

Solemn Assemblies Shift Outcomes

God responds to consecrated gatherings marked by repentance, fasting, and humility. Solemn assemblies create divine moments where heaven intervenes and national outcomes are shifted. (Joel 2; Esther 4)

4

United Prayer Releases Authority

United prayer and fasting dismantle spiritual strongholds and confront governing principalities over nations. Through agreement, God's people receive divine strategy and enforce heaven's authority on the earth. (Daniel 9)

5

We Set the Table for Unity

We intentionally cultivate unity across generations, denominations, cultures, and nations. Unity carries God's commanded blessing and multiplies the power of prayer and proclamation. (Psalm 133; Psalm 68:11)

6

We Live With Future Generations in View

We build with a generational vision, stewarding obedience today for inheritance tomorrow. Our labor ensures that truth, testimony, and spiritual foundations are preserved for those yet to be born. (Psalm 102:18)

THE PRAYERS OF A MILLION MOTHERS

"I answered the prayers of a million mothers." — The Lord to Francis Frangipane

In studying the history of revivals, Francis Frangipane noticed something striking: every revival was preceded by an organized prayer movement—except one. Curious, he asked the Lord why. The Lord replied, "I answered the prayers of a million mothers."

"Blow the trumpet, consecrate a fast, gather the people" — the gathering called every generation to the altar, with no one exempt.

In September 2000, after God miraculously provided for the work, 400,000 young adults gathered on the National Mall in Washington D.C. for a Joel 2-style solemn assembly of fasting and prayer — modeled after heaven's prescription for a nation in crisis.

The Lord answered the prayers of mothers across generations, and that cry continues to echo across Africa today. A Million Women Africa stands on that same promise: that united prayer, fasting, and consecration will shift the destiny of nations.
